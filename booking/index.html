Booking & fees

My fees

Fees are on a sliding scale of £55–£65 based on what you can afford (an initial in person introduction session is £20)

I strongly believe that counselling should be accessible to all. Therefore if you would like to discuss fees or anything else please just ask me about this.

Each session lasts for 50 minutes.

You can pay for sessions in cash or via bank transfer.

My location

I practice out of two Hove locations:
at The Wilbury Clinic and Hove Therapy Rooms. Both are in Hove.
